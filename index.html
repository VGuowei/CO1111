Welcome to the research experiment!

What is this about?

In this experiment participants will have to try out two provided websites, test site A: Organized UI which have all related items grouped together appropriately and test site B: Unorganized UI the opposite. Then they will submit a review for which one they prefer to use. To begin select either A Test or B Test.

Privacy

No private data is needed. Any information provided will be anonymized and available on their local machine, participants can withdraw at any moment without submitting a review. The collected data will be deleted when the research project comes to an end or manually in the local machine.
